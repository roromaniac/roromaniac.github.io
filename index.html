About Me

I recently wrapped up my coursework as a PhD student at the Univerity of Michigan - Ann Arbor and should shortly receive my MS in Statistics. My current research is about brining interpretable and visual friendly results for data generated by spatial transcriptomics. My advisor is Dr. Jeff Regier and Dr. Jackson Loper is another major collaborator on the project. My current research interests are:

  • Application of Graph Neural Nets to Spatial Transcriptomics Data
  • Bayesian Computation
  • Probabilistic Dimension Reduction
  • Application of Hierarchial Models
